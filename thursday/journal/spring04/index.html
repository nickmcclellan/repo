My second half of Junior Year was just about the most depressing semester ever, but somehow, I consider it the best Spring ever. After coming back from a dismal Christmas, I adopted my ambitious "New Year's Project" and set out to make this Spring different from all the others. Submitted for your approval, it's up to you to see if I really succeeded. Be sure to check out the first entry, my mission statement "New Year's Project." They all rock, really, you should just read them all. They get better every semester.

At the beginning of my third year at college, I had finally found my place. I got an apartment off campus, remained close to my best friends, and fell hopelessly in love with Matt. While I continued to struggle with classes, I nonetheless worked very hard and bested some difficult courses. I went from party star to dutiful SO staying at home on a Saturday night—it was nuts. I experienced my most difficult year: I lost my car, my cell phone busted, and my computer bit the big one. I grew up a whole lot that year. Make sure you check out the year that may not have turned many heads but still managed to be my favorite.
