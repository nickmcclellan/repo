Tuesday, February 15, 2005

Amateurs

My professor thinks I don't know what I want to do. He's probably right.

Is that a bad thing? I don't really think so. I have my eye on this Media Matters internship, but maybe that's just because my eyes don't have many places to go. I think I'm going to talk to Dr. Martin again about the whole thing. Maybe after I have a few more clips and experience under my belt, I won't feel so worthless sitting in front of him.

This isn't a bad thing, trust me. Worrying about what I'm going to do after school is not going to make any difference. Just to be safe, I e-mailed the Flagpole to see about any copy editing things I might be able to do. How studious am I??

I think I'm going to start on the Freshman Blog soon. Basically, for every semester, I'm going to do a little Blog retrospective, with reflections on the year. I think the Freshman Blog is going to be the most fun to do. What sucked about freshman year? Absolutely nothing (except for that day I puked all over my bed and got pulled over when I was on my way to Wal-Mart to get Resolve carpet cleaner). I may even go back and modernize the old Blogs and fix up the massive HTML errors. Oh, what work there's to be done...

I have to write my first paper tonight. I'm not looking forward to it. I'm going to do some cardio w/ Jerm at the gym just to get away from it for a few hours.

Tomorrow, I'm going out to lunch with Leena. I even got my hair cut for her. It looks very mod—that's the first thing I said.

It appears this Blog entry has collapsed in on itself like a neutron star, so I'm going to head to class now...listening to Japanese mp3's...but to you, it won't appear so, because I have reported something different!

It appears today is not your day.
