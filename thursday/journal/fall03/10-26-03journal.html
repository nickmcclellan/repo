a waste, a loss

I got into ENGL4800!! For all those idiots out there, that's Advanced Creative Writing. I guess I'm not an idiot afterall. I was kinda half-expecting the professor to e-mail me saying my poetry was too personal to be good. I can see that, and I want to move away from such things, it's just that I feel so inspired by emotion, ESPECIALLY negative emotion. It's like venting. I want to write more stuff that would have an impact on more than just myself. Modernism gets me more than I had originally thought, so maybe if I went through some Wallace Stevens and some William Carlos Williams I could be all literary. Wouldn't that be cool. I think it's the one thing I can stand to do because I do it anyways.

I think I'll go to Cingular now and see if they can fix my poor cell phone. If they can't, expect me begging some people for phone numbers. Yah...fun times...ANYWAYS, it's always those phone numbers that you never call but never want to delete that are the ones that you cherish the most. I mean, what if that number calls you? Then you can see the caller ID and be all like, "Wow, that's the person I never call...interesting!" Let's hear it for rarely called phone numbers, folks. Alright.
