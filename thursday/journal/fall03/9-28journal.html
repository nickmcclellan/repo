A shadow beneath me
Covets, persistant and needing
hold me still
release me

the earth kisses
holding a dead man, for a moment
before he sees that which kills him
pushed him beneath

"Release me," he said, smiling.
hearing him, I could forget my struggle
tossing him a penny--
a beat--I could only make him smile

and all this trouble
because I
loved
him...
