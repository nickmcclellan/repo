I guess it all starts tomorrow, huh? I am hopefully going to resolve this scheduling problem by 8am tomorrow. After that I guess I have to join the rest of the world and start purchasing books and studying. I guess we all can't live in these dream worlds where there is no work to be done, all play. Nope, responcibility here I come!

Entertaining, and kinda funny, in a morbidly depressing way. You know what's morbidly depressing, this book I'm reading. I finally picked up The Heart is Deceitful Above All Things by J. T. Sigh...the Pad only wishes she was that cool (but you KNOW you are Pad!)

Enough banter for now...I will be sleeping now...goodnight!

Thursday, January 16th

I guess this is inevitable, so I'll make it brief...or I'll try. I'm titling my journal this week Separation for obvious reasons, and some that are good, you know...because I'm an up kind of person, so there has to be something optimistic, you know? Because I am NEVER Paranoid...never, and I think that is so cool. I know, I'm not an idiot, I'm very keen and I can read the writing that's on the wall.

Yes...you can understand me. If you try, certainly you know. Yes.

Separation because from this moment on it's me and me only. There's nobody standing behind me or next to me. My feelings are my own now, and that's good. That's all I can find that's good in this. I don't want to think about that though. I don't want to think about anything. I just want to do something that's going to make this any bit like how I thought it would be. I was so naive and insepid. I'm such a fucking fool. It happened last year, you just let yourself go, thinking that maybe it's that time, because that time of year is so fucked up anyways. And it was, and I believed it, I always believe it. So trusting, so very innocent. No intention, no intervention, nothing. Just that chord repeated over and over again, thinking that maybe it's what I need this time, maybe it's something more, maybe something, but that involves thinking, and that's not the thing I need now. I couldn't find Missy, but I think she's coming now, maybe, hopefully, I mean, wouldn't she be here for something like this?

She was here...she's cool, yah, I'm going to watch "Becoming" with her. What am I supposed to do now? I don't want to sleep in the dark place, but I suppose I have to, I have to. I don't have to eat for days. But I have to sleep, and all. I'm strong, and I will be alright. I climb mountains.

Friday, January 17th

Sorry I made such a vauge and completely mindless entry, but I was in a bad spot, and I kinda still am. I had very strange dreams last night (Note that I had dreams...I know I have them all the time, but I rarely remember them). In one, I kept dying over and over and was being chased by a government agency who kept bringing me back in a new body because they had to preserve some sort of secret. The other dream...I don't remember.

Well, the point of this all is Bryant told me that he didn't feel the same way about me that I felt about him...anymore. I had sensed this...every since last Wednesday, I knew. It was a scary thought because I really liked him and I didn't want to continue feeling that way if he didn't feel the same way. It just sucked...it was like the good thing about all of this mess was instantly gone and it was overwhelming. It also meant that my perceptions about the whole situation were correct. This is a good indication that any further perceptions of mine are probably true, so thus I had to end whatever I had felt for him...and that was hard. I realized that it wasn't anything that I had for him, it was all raw potential. I had capped it last Wednesday, realizing that I shouldn't go too far. He kept it vaguely clear that he had plateued. Anyways, the sudden loss of all that possibility was just really a fucking let-down. I wanted to get drunk tonight with Missy (One of many things I wanted to do) but I'm not going to get drunk feeling all down like this! I can't imagine what I would act like. My friends are all being so very cool to me, Vicki, Missy, John, Sonya, Gen, and Allyson...they all try their best, and ultimatly, my way to feeling better is through my friends. It just sucks now, but separation is good.

Something I mentioned before to people is I hate being subjugated, and that's how I felt alot of the time. Separation gets me away from this...I stay away and start to remember just how I was before. I think I was in that whole, "I don't want to date for a while" mode...and after dumping that and finding someone I could really get into, it's kinda hard to get back into that. I feel like I want a rebound, but that's NOT what I'm going to do. I think I'm just going to have to live. "The hardest thing to do in this world is to live in it. Live." I'm going to have to struggle through my crap semester, read all these things, write all these papers, make my grades, all by myself. That sucks, but I'm going to do it. I have to. And when things get hard, my friends are there for me. Why does Spring semester always have to suck?
