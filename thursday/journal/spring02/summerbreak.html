Well, it is very nice to finally have been completely freed of all my obligations back in Athens. I have been working every day since last Thursday, but luckily I got my first day off on Wednesday. Last night at 12:02, I got to see Star Wars Episode 2, and it was very enjoyable. If you want my opinion, it wasn't as good as it could have been. It's full of Star Wars type thrills and tons of political intrigue. It's interesting trying to find out who is the bad guy, because that is very difficult. I think that George Lucas is saving the big surprises until Episode 3, which, because of lack of development this time around, will be where Anakin Skywalker becomes Darth Vader. All in all, I thought it was an excellent film, you'll definitely want to check it out.
