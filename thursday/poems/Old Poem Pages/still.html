by:  Nick McClellan

 

A shadow beneath me

Covets, persistent and needing

hold me still

release me

 

the earth kisses

holding a dead man, for a moment

before he sees that which kills him

pushed him beneath

 

“Release me,” he said, smiling.

hearing him I could only forget my struggle

tossing him a spot—

a beat—I could only make him smile

 

and all this trouble

because I

loved

him…
