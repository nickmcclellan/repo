This is a test

I'm feeling like days are the most pressing inconvenience. I find them increasingly pointless, and I look forward to the time that I am sleeping in spite of them, or sleeping at the end of them. I should be working on my articles, but I don't want to. I don't want to, and I won't. I want to sit here, maybe type this out, and then go upstairs and sleep on a chair. I want to sit there and take whatever it is I'm supposed to take until it's time for something else, and whatever else after that, until it's time to rest again.

I feel like I'm just killing time. I just want to feel noticed. I want to feel honored, remembered, and respected. In its absense, I feel pointless. The gaping holes in the road that were once so easy to transverse are now pulling me into them. I feel like I need help with this, but I don't know who to turn to. They're either too far, too involved, or don't care whatsoever. I feel like sheading the skin and every part of me to get back what has been taken from me. I feel it's so important, I can't breathe without it. I feel this loss is so pointless and unnecessary, that if I had one moment to trample the beast, it would be no more. I feel surmounting this obstacle and putting it behind me will make all of these other diversions nothing more than daily nusances. Despite my confidence, I am powerless to stop what is coming, and I don't know what to do because of it.

In this absense, I feel like a shell. I know I should be stronger, and that I should demand more than this, but I am so willing to do anything I can for this person, that demanding his apology would only undermine what I have to offer. I can forgive, turn the other cheek, and be the one to sacrifice. In the meantime, I expect some sort of salvation to await my deed, but so far I've come up empty handed, and the realist inside me tends to doubt the legitimacy of such a feat. I feel as if I'm falling, but I'm not sure how far the rabbit hole goes, and I don't know how long I'll be down there. I don't know what's there to know, and most of all, I don't know what to expect when I come out of this. Is this person going to be there to help me out of this? Will I be down there to help him? Or is what I fear most the most certain:

I am alone.
