Writing

I have always had a desire to write. My main objective for obtaining my English degree was to sharpen my critical thinking and writing skills.

The writings samples I have compiled convey some of my academic, creative and professional objectives in writing. As you can see, I do not limit myself to one style.

As a lifetime writer, I hope to continue to sharpen my skills.

The Emerging Gay and Lesbian Media: This was my third term paper completed for my Journalism Ethics class with Professor Conrad Fink. I explored the relationship the media has to the gay and lesbian community.
