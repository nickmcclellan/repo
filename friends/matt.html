This is Matt Stephens. We started dating on May 5, 2003 after a fateful trip to Atlanta to drop of the dorm stuff at the house of one of his friends. That night, four of us piled into bed together, and Matt kissed me, and I began what was one of the most amazing experiences of my life. We were together for almost a year and a half.

In September of 2004, Matt decided it was time for him to leave. He wanted to escape Athens, which he blamed for his depression, and he sought to start over in Atlanta. He also admitted that he wanted to go even farther. I had a very difficult time with his decision to leave me and Athens, but despite my pleas, I said good-bye to him in January, 2005.

I still love Matt, and he is my friend. I will always have a special place for him. I haven't spoken to him since January, but I hope that one day he will speak to me again.

The very first time I heard this song, I thought of Matt. It's a special song for me and it's one of my favorite songs...
