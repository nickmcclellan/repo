Sophomore Year, Missy and I brought the fun to Mary Lyndon. Those were the days.

Missy certainly had a flair for breaking those pesky sexual boundaries. Nowadays, Missy and I just marvel at the fact that we are still close friends and still find that those bright-eyed freshmen we once were are still a part of us.

This is one person I'm not getting away from. We're friends for life, yo.
