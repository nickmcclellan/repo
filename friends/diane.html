This is Diane. Yes, I know, she has a bug on her head. This doesn't typically happen with Diane. She has pleasant, bug-free hair most of the time.

Diane and I have been ESOL co-teachers during my Senior year. I was having dinner at her apartment one night and she told me that she was going to an info session and I told her it sounded like fun. I had known Diane for a few years, but I never got the chance to get to know her. I mean, there was that one time we were at this Halloween party and I drove her home. I'd like to think we really made a connection that evening. Oh, yah, and there was that other party when we talked in Spanish to eachother while everyone looked at us dumbfounded. Yeah, we rock.

She's really dedicated at what she does and she's going to be a really sexy teacher one day. I hear she wants to move to Australia or something, which is neat. You know, for one there are lots more bugs that can perch themselves in her hair, but also because I know she's going to go see some amazing things. In the time that I've known her, she's made some kick ass lesson plans. All the students love her (especially the eligible bachelors), and she just drives us all crazy with her attempts to dress conservatively.

Diane has been such fun to teach with! I also credit her with introducing me to hummus. Vegetarians rock! :-)
