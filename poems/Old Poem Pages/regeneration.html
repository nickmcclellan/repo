By:  Nick McClellan

 

Come on!

You seek to hurt me

Tear me apart!

I invite it.

 

Ah, a severed arm

Falls defeated to the ground

Weekend spent by windows

Tears fall outside of me

Return Monday to shake your hand.

 

That’s it?

I’m only happy when it rains

More, I dare you!

Let’s see what you got

 

Arrow in the chest

Finally got what you wanted

Blood bursts and falls

But it stops and breaks

And falls away

 

Scars are like money

Can you ever have enough?

You knocked me down

But it made me stronger

 

Broken heart

Am I supposed to yield?

Acid kisses can penetrate

Change the nature of love

Repair me

 

Sigh, no real challenge

I thought you were special

I thought you would know

So, do it!

 

I’m waiting, glowing with power

Bullets bounce up off me

Poison tastes exquisite

Man slaughter’s child’s play

C’mon, prove me wrong

 

Just try.
