Crashed

Well, this journal entry is possibly the most interesting of any journal entry I've done. Ok, let me preface this alittle...

I had all ready a whole perfect week of journal entries...BUT my damn computer got a virus and crashed. Because I lost every program, including FrontPage, I'm having to do this entry in a primative wordpad file. This is very odd...no spell check...no background...I'm not even sure it will work. Well, I'm giving it a try.

Well, I will go more into what happened this week later...I had a birthday, it was nice...blah blah. I'm going home tomorrow to get some programs I need...like Word. After I have all that stuff back, I'll have normal journals again...I pondered creating a live journal, but cringed at the idea of falling into the conformist majority...I stand by my feelings about how vain internet journals are, but I say this because I am admitting to it. I'm guilty of making this some sick little game of what can I write to make people interested. Like I said, I'm going to really analyze why it is I do this in the first place.

Last night I behaved like a complete idiot. It has lead me to throw down the bottle for a few weeks. God, I really need to deal with my feelings in a different way then getting drunk and having fun...it's not going to make me feel better, in fact, I've felt like I was going to throw up all day long. Disgusting... I was mean to John last night, and I apologized...but I will admit, there are reasons...drunken reasons based on sober thought, I swear. I'm an open book people, I don't see why you can't see it already. Whatever, whatever...ask me if you really want to know, and I might tell you. But then I might not...oh well. Despite everything, I feel really bad for being a brat to John. It won't happen again.

For those that don't know, I broke up with Aiman on Tuesday. That was hard, but it was the right thing to do. Seriously, I want to get into this more, but I feel like doing it in my regular Frontpage environment instead on in this strange freaky notepad application. I'm going to Kronos Quartet with him tonight though, as friends. I guess things may be akward for a while, but it's for the better, I swear.

Nicole sent me a letter this week, I was very happy to recieve this. She made alot of really good points to me and made me realize alot. I told her about my issues and my issues with Aiman, and she suggested that maybe I should take some time out. She said I should take some time to "fall in love with myself again." I like that. I am liking the term "aloof" lately. I just don't care, seriously. I really don't. My life doesn't revolve around whether or not I have a boyfriend, and I'm not looking for a rebound at all. I like to think I don't need to define myself by the person I'm dating. I'm pretty level-headed most of the time. I want to date someone I like...whatever that means. As far as having a boyfriend, I want to do that with someone I just feel so gravitated to...I don't forsee that happening with anyone I know though. Sigh...it would be nice, that's the dream, but I'm totally not desperate for it. I realize that the guy I'm going to fall for will show up eventually...it's just a matter of time. I have a very big heart, and it will happen. Until then, I'm just going to enjoy life and stay sane.
