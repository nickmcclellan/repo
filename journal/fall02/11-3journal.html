"Silence is Golden"

Saturday, November 2nd

I probably shouldn't have uploaded my journal early this morning since technically this entry belongs with it...but fuck it, I don't care. I feel like I want to write less in this journal. I don't see why I write in it at all these days. I am too much of a chicken shit to share how I really feel. I mean, I whine about shit not happening and make vain attempts at profound writing, all stemming from this insane notion that someone is actually reading this... I miss the days when I would forget to write in this thing all week long and have to construct some substantive blurp before uploading it (probably sometime late Sunday night...always late). I'm going to make an effort to save up what I want to write about...why the hell does someone need to know the tiny details of my everyday life, you don't care anyways. You'd probably want to hear what I want to tell you instead of what I am too cowardly to even refer to by name. Fuck all of that, things need to change.

I'm sick of my stupid patterns. I can be so damn obsessive, it makes me want to rip my arms off and beat myself with them. It's not healthy, and it's so boring walking the line between that shadow on the wall and that crazy loon all up in your face. Fuck all of that, right? Who cares anymore? I'm over it...I'm over it...I just keep throwing around in my head the truth...that solid, opaque and unmistakable truth...that pessimistic reality that I've been so afraid to see. It's like an electric fence...it hurts, but it keeps me grounded...keeps me from leaving my mud hole. It's not so bad here. I'm sane here, and I feel like myself.

You don't want to hear all this, do you? What the fuck does it all mean? NOTHING...it means NOTHING, and that's all it should be to anyone, and I will try my best to focus not on that, but on other things...

Today, I did stuff...but I'm not going to share EVERYTHING I did because nobody wants to hear that, right? So true. Well, related to my above blurp of nothingness...I saw April tonight. I spent the evening hours with her at her house, and I told about some stuff...not everything. I told her stuff, she didn't quite understand, but that's ok. I needed some April time. She reminds me of what I came from. We had a nice time, ate some food...she shared some very personal poems with me...poems of a nature I won't share with anyone else. She knows me...and that's refreshing. It's nice to have someone to come home to that still recognizes the things inside that make you who you are.

Sunday, November 3rd...12:57am

God, I can't even stop writing even when I resolve not to do it. What do you expect though? I'm an English major. I've been writing my whole life, and nothing's going to stop me.

I still can't get over it...over anything. That opaque and solid truth, just falling down throughout my head, it's under things, I don't want to look at it. I pull it out sometimes...it's scary...but I can't stand but look through it. God, I can't help but excuse my foolish dreams...why can't I just let it die...why do I have to hold tight onto that one chance...it's not love.

I don't know what love is. I won't lie, I've never been in love. God, I could do it though. I could be the most amazing lover. My world just fits so perfectly around that one person...that guy that's just going to know...everything...that's just going to break through every wall. I feel that love is so precious...and lately, I feel like I really do have only a handful of shots to get it right. Am I just going to know or can he just pop up, and I just suddenly, "Ok, yah, I can love you now..."

I watched that episode of Queer as Folk tonight...that one from this summer with Fab Filippo...the one where Brian neglects Justin, and Justin goes to "violin guy" for the song he was promised. Before all of this, that scene...it was just electric, I could feel that. That spontaneous eclectic hunger for someone...I could feel that guy just needed Justin, and he didn't care how he got him, but he just knew that he could. He knew that love was just there, and SNAP, it happened! Well, I also felt so into that scene because Fab Filippo is so hot...I tried to grow alittle facial hair like him this summer...it was a failed attempt...

The glass is half empty, in this case, and I need to get used to that. It's hard though...it's hard when all you want to do is love...and all you want to do is fucking feel SOMETHING...and all you want to do is...SNAP!

Saturday, November 9th...2:05am

I'm anxious to send this journal out...I feel if it's uploaded, then I won't feel like I need to write something. I did well at keeping it very lite...I can't communicate how much I am beginning to HATE internet journals. Everyone has one, and it's making us so lazy. We can't even communicate with eachother, we have to write these silly little words down onto some dark corner of the internet just because we are too cowardly to say what we feel. In my own defense, I'll own up to anything I write here...I am never lying. But those are just excuses...I really should just stop, step back, and really think about why I do this in the first place.

She made me feel very good.

Tomorrow night I'm going to party my ass off at Boneshakers...and I'm not going to walk in with a dime...and everyone better buy me a drink for my birthday, or else I'm going to be all heartbroken...or just a very bitter sober person. Ok, I'm going to go to bed now, goodnight...
