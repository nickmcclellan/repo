Tuesday, December 3rd...2:09AM

I have always been transfixed by the stars. I find myself, in the most interesting moments, just staring up at the sky, feeling like I can see every piece of heaven. I am walking back to my dorm tonight, and I look up, and I see Orion. I think of when I saw it rising on the horizon, the Earth spinning in that short spance of time, watching it rise in the East...that most pleasant eastern horizon...going home. I saw it tonight, and I moved from Orion to those other nameless stars, the mysteries of the sky. I see one bright and brilliant star, and suddenly I can see hundreds around that one, and still more when I swing around to look up behind me ...they're everywhere! I can't help but see them...and all I want to do is fall to the ground and spend hours exploring the sky ...until finally I can see them all.
