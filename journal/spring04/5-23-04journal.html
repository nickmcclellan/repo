Anyways, just thought I'd share an Ohio story. Not sure if it's common knowledge, but my mother works at the tissue bank in Dayton. The tissue bank is where they bring all you generous organ donors after you die so they can cut you up and harvest your organs, veins, and bones for science. So Saturday night, Mike is looking for a notary so I can have the Prix's title signed over to me and we end up at the Dayton morgue. I was actually in the garage that the ambulances must bring the dead bodies! On the way out, my mom even opened the fridge door for a moment to give me a peak at some of the fresher specimens. FREAKY!

The most intriguing exchange came about when Mike took us to his office so he could notarize the title. Some guy was there, apprantly relegated that evening to manning the morgue. Mom asked him about this guy they brought in last week. "He looked like a wild man!" she exclaimed, as he touted some interesting tattoes. Mister morgue duty agreed, saying they found him some barn in the middle of no-where where he hung himself.

"We found one hand in the noose--it looked like he was having second thoughts," he said.

Well, I guess it was too late :-x

A 50-year-old Zambian man has hanged himself after his wife found him having sex with a hen, police said Friday.

The woman caught him in the act when she rushed into their house to investigate a noise.

"He attempted to kill her but she managed to escape," a police spokesman said.

The man from the town of Chongwe, about 50 km (30 miles) east of Lusaka, killed himself after being admonished by other villagers.

The hen was slaughtered after the incident.
