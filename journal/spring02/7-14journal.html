Week of July 14th, 2002

Monday, July 15th

I've noticed lately that I am having alot of that pent up creative energy lately. I'm finding that I'm spear-heading my website more lately then I typically do. It's odd, because when I started this thing, I was so into it, and I actually made a whole different version of the site before settling on what I have now. By the time I discovered exactly how I wanted to deal with the site, I had been sucked dry of that whole creative fire, and I found myself making all these pages that I didn't want to make because I was just not into it anymore. Like, I had committed to making pages for some of my friends, but I was so sick of spending a good 20 minutes on these things because it wasn't thrilling anymore at that time.

I find that now I'm being revisited by that whole trilling thing about making the page, so it's really cool. I like working on what I'm planning for when I go back to school. I have a really cool link bar and the galleries page is nice too. I'm really into what I'm planning for post-Christmas! I'm thinking of doing an 8-part webseries for my comic book character Sherry Cherry that I made up in 7th grade. I am thinking that maybe into this fall I can write and draw the comics and when I go back home for Christmas I can scan and prepare the sites to be published. Then over a few weeks in the spring I can publish them and maybe have some sort of cult following, wouldn't that be awesome! I guess I would have to stick on them that they are not to be copied without my written position, that's very important. I'll have to see, alot of things I have to work out with that. I think I'd have to do a bigger format so my chicken scratch can be read on the screen, so I'll be sure to let you all know if that's going to fly in the spring.

I'm anxiously awaiting word on who my roommate is to be at UGA in the fall. I'm hoping he's going to be alittle of an improvement from my last roommate, who, I MUST say was excellent, but I'm ready for someone different. Ideally, I would like to room with someone I can relate to as more then a roommate/acquaintance. It would be awesome to have a cool friend I can do stuff with, but I'm certainly not expecting it. I'm just hoping he's going to be open minded, NOT smelly, and willing to venture out of the dorm, because GOD KNOWS I need my private time.

Well, I'm working this week every morning except for Wednesday and Saturday, so I must get my sleep. I'll touch base later on if something interesting happens...doesn't look promising...damn...adios folks.
