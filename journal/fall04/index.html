My final year at UGA was the most dark and depressing of all my years at UGA. Matt decided to leave Athens behind, and me along with it. I became very depressed and my website and Blog suffered. Despite Matt's decision, I was eventually able to soldier through it. My final semester at UGA was challenging, but ultimately a success. I got to see my favorite band in concert, reconnect to some of my best friends, and I quietly prepared what would be my final update for my website. At the end of the summer, I was able to leave Athens behind. Please read the Senior Year Blog to learn how it all came to an end.
