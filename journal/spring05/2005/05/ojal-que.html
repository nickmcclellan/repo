"Thursday"

Wednesday, May 18, 2005

Ojalá que

I just got back from Padmini's, watching the movie La Mala Educación. It was good, I was pleasantly surprised that I liked it. I was anticipating a story about two women, for some reason, and it turned out to be filled to the brim with gay men. It was really creepy though, with lots of crossdressers and pedophiles. I guess that's Almodóvar's style. It was also pseudo-film noir, which was somewhat disconcerting. Like the time I watched The Big Sleep, I was left with so many questions. I guess that means it did the job. Kudos for that.

I'm going to see Revenge of the Sith with Kyle and Paul tomorrow. Gen was supposed to come, but she's got some other plans and had to cancel. I'm going to have to find someone to go instead of her or else I'll just pay for her unused ticket. Maybe I can scalp it? I hope this film hasn't been blown that far out of proportion that people need to buy extras from me. That would be too much. I mean, who wouldn't want to see the making of Darth Vader? I only have to see it once and I'll be satisfied.

I feel like running every day of the week now. I don't have anything better to do. Just find a job. I think I will make a new website for myself this week. Just a small one to send to employers. I know I can do it. I've also decided that upon that wonderful day that I do find myself employed, I will be cutting my hair. It's been long for over a year and now I want to get a buzz cut. I feel like all of those celebrities I was trying to emulate have dropped their sexy locks for something much shorter. Maybe I should follow.

Anyways, tonight I had some tofu pasta with Padmini and Jerm. It's going to be sad when I leave Padmini behind and Jerm has left the both of us to learn Russian in Vermont. They're just about the best friends anyone could have. I mean, Jerm knows his bubbly and Padmini bakes a delicious loaf of garlic toast. They rock.

I'm editing the Junior Blog, which means I will be writing the Senior Blog soon. I want it to be really good. I feel like the Junior Blog was so hard to write because I was so happy then and it's hard to approach a time like then when I feel now so close to those emotions. I had such a good time writing the Freshman and Sophomore Blogs because I was comfortable being nostalgic of the past because I had let it go. Not the Junior Blog, though. No, that's still very much alive for me. I tried my best to be honest, though. Hopefully it will be a worthy addition to the site.

I should sleep. I plan on running at 10 a.m. Thank God for the summer sun.
