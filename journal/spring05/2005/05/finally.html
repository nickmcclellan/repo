"Thursday"

Wednesday, May 04, 2005

Finally

I'm feeling very strange. Who really cares about school anymore? I mean, all you dedicated undergrads with one or two more years left to suffer through, I suppose I can understand that, but I'm so over it now. I've secured my A in English, attended the Fink farewell, and I've studiously sent out my resume to several potential employers.

I sent one today to a paper in Pennsylvania, 90 minutes from New York and Boston. I know, I know, it might not be glamorous, but I think it's close enough to civilization for me to tool around for about 2 years before I hit up a major metropolitan daily. I won't mention the two jobs I applied for in NYC—they're long shots w/ big time publishers, but I know I can do the job. I just hope all this good karma I have starts working its magic. A Brandy face, any day now...

Other than all that, I have some editing to do before my portfolio is due tomorrow. I know I can do it, I just don't care very much about it. Like I said, I just don't care anymore. There's a final Friday. Afterward, Gen's going somewhere to pick up a puppy. I think I'll tag along. Friday night, it's the last Krush Girls, for me at least. I think I'm going to do it. I'm going to get all tipsy and cute and dance like the brave little toaster that I know I am, and I'm going to pretend everything is good again. Maybe, for one night, it will be enough just to pretend :-)

After that, nothing! A whole week before graduation and all I have to do is pester companies to pay me money in exchange for my valuable service. My mom is coming next week, so I think I'll have some stuff to do. My cousin and aunt are coming to see me graduate. I bought my cap and gown the other day to show off to them. Just so you know, it wasn't cheap! I had to buy two tassels because I didn't know which degree to tout—English or Journalism. I got two degrees, so I figure I deserve two tassels.

Monday night was the Red & Black end of semester shindig at Taco Stand. I went, recalling awkward cast parties from that musical I did back in high school. I didn't "belong" there, I don't know many of the people. I knew enough to be welcome, at least. That's one thing I did this year that I should be really proud of. Ever since I was a freshman, I wanted to be on staff at the Red & Black. I accomplished that this semester, and that's pretty cool. I wrote headlines, positioned some dueling mugs, and had my name published each and every day. How much does that rock!

Running goes well. I do it faster now, didn't you know? Well I do. Apparently, running shirtless doesn't tan me enough. Fuck it. If I have to be pale, I don't care. I tried.

Also, Marilia's birthday is today! Happy Birthday girl. I'm sending her a care package (don't worry, she knows cause I told her when she talked to me for an hour on Tuesday night). It will be a surprise. I hope it makes her happy.

I think I'll go to bed early and edit in the morning. Send me all your job wishes! I hope to settle that before I go to Europe. Then, all will be well.

Maybe I'll put up my poem tomorrow...if Padmini workshops it in time. Expect that very soon. Later.
