Laura's Thesis

Saturday, I got the chance to be a part of my cousin's thesis. She's taking pictures of lots of her friends and family, putting them in situations that best captures their personalities (though her teacher tells her she'll never be able to truly capture her subject's true personality).

Anyways, she was nice enough to give me the technical shots from what we did Saturday. I've always wanted my cousin to take pictures of me. She did a phenomenal job, and on top of that, I have some really stunning pictures of me. No more of that tired old picture from high school graduation.

Expect a more detailed entry eventually...tonight I'm off.
