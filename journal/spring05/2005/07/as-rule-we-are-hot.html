Thursday, July 28, 2005

As a Rule, We Are Hot

For my last week of Blogging, I'm sending out a few tributes. There is a small handful of people that have made my last semester in Athens worthwhile. The first little tribute went out to my good friend Jeremy. I got Marilia down when she left for Chile. Missy got hers this week. This one belongs to Leena.

Last night, I got a call from Leena. She wanted to go downtown, one last time before we went out separate ways. It's funny, I was looking forward to sitting at home and watching Kill Bill Vol. 2 for the third time, but somehow that all melted away when I heard she was going out. If it was anyone else, I would have just told them, "Some other time," but for Leena, I'd go. That girl is worth it.

I was passed out for most the late afternoon because I had been moving furniture all day. Let me just say I'm very happy to be rid of my washer and dryer. God, those things were heavy. When I woke up and talked to Leena, I jumped in the shower and hurried my little butt to her apartment so we could get going.

The finer details of the evening are not important. We went to a few bars, had alot to drink, and shared some thoughts. We came to the decision that we couldn't refuse compliments given to eachother. She thinks I'm hot and I won't correct her. I think she's just fucking amazing, and she can't argue with me about it.

I don't know if there's anything more I can say than this: I haven't been happy this year. It's hard to find the motivation to be the patient and adventurous person I used to be. I told Leena last night that, more than anyone else, spending time with her (whether it be at the front of the stage at a Krush Girls show or it be a cool spring afternoon lunch at the Grit) has made me the happiest I have been this year. When I'm with her, I forget my stupid problems and just have about as much fun as possible.

It's sad to say, but I would have never expected to be so close to Leena. We drifted away for a few years but came back together. I'm really going to miss her alot, but I'm sure she'll learn lots cutting up cadavers.

Hey, that's one way I can get into MCG...
