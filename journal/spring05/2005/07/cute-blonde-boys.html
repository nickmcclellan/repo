Cute Blonde Boys

This will be deleted as soon as I wake up. The only reason it exists is that there was one part of me that wanted to talk to him, but I let him slip away (and walk away from me). I regret that. I hope someone sees this (especially special persons who I intend to bike with tomorrow late afternoon).

This is how I really feel, though, so listen...

*sigh*...why can't i talk to cute blond boys socializing a mere few feet from me?

and blonde is spelt with an E, obviously :-\

I know I told him that once, someplace.

It's too bad I'm such a nerd. If I wasn't such a nerd, maybe I would have told him I had been crushing him all year. I'm such a stupid dork...
