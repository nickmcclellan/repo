Obnoxious, But Cute

Sorry for this, really, but I had fun with Leena and Dille tonight! I also saw Jackie, which was really strange since I just got over saying goodbye to her last week. I pressured her for that damned Friends Page picture! Any day now and all those will be finished...

One thing before I get my much deserved 4 hours of sleep: I don't think I got ugly in these four years of college. In fact, I think I got cuter. Granted, I may be older and may even look it, but I could never have hoped to run 6 miles four days in a row when I was a pimple-faced freshman. No way, no how.

So, yah, gawk at my cuteness. I know I'm hot.

(But Leena's hotter)
