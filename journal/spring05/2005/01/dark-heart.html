Monday, January 24, 2005

Dark Heart

I've been pretty numb lately. I admit that I have Senioritus, and I'm not really giving classes as much emphasis as I probably should be. I mean, it's the third week, I have some things to do, but considering all I've been through, I just know things are going to be alright. I was playing "what if" with my mother this weekend, telling her that if I failed my Women and Chaucer class, I'd still have a degree in Newspapers. She wasn't amused—but come on! Am I going to fail?? I don't think so.

Basically, I've been plugging away at Friends Pages. I'd like to have the revamped section up by Friday. That would be nice. Right now, I have nine pages left to do—two of which I'm creating. I e-mailed Mary-Kate and told her I wanted a picture of her so I could introduce my website to her glorious presence. She's not around Athens anymore, and I really miss her.

It always turns out that after I've labored so strenuously on an update, I decide that my website is inadequate :-/ I mean, I really need to practice my CSS, this site is good, granted, I mean, it looks pretty amazing. I've never done anything as amazing as this, but I could do so much more with CSS. I wouldn't have to labor as much, and I might just inch closer and closer to the 21st century. I think I may have to buy a book or maybe screw with some of my pages, playing around. Who knows if the final update will be CSS. I might make it exclusively HTML—just because this website is based on a very simple design. I mean, when I'm out of this place and I purchase my own webspace (like Jerm!), I think I'll set the bar alittle higher. For now, I think I'll just play around with CSS. There's no rush.

I was thinking about my cousin's page last week. I was thinking, in the interest of building my resume, I can make that page look just phenomenal. It would take alot of work, but it really is alot of fun. I love working with my webpage and such, but working with content that I have less control over is just exhilarating. I mean, it's like I look at her photography, and I'm like, "What kind of a webpage does this make?" If you haven't checked it out, you must see the Photography of Laura Jones.

The weight-training hell continues. I was in so much pain this weekend, but it finally subsided by Sunday. I went back today, but who knows if I will be miserable tomorrow. Let's just say, I can't bench press crap. My partner was like, "Take the bar down, and then explode up!" Unfortunately, all my explosions were spent, and he did most of the lifting :-/

He did say one thing. He said I'm going to be so strong by the end of the semester. I sure hope so, I'm in the mood to do something amazing—I want to change my life.

In other news, I'm a vegetarian now. Surprise, surprise.
