Wednesday, January 12, 2005

Mad World

I've had a difficult week. It all started with something very troubling. If I've noticed anything lately, it's that I have been almost desperate to cut myself off from people. I have been riding my bike around school, and I do so with the iPod, but I try not to look at people. I just want to be here, do what it is I have to do, and go home again. Genevieve is pretty cool though. She told me that her New Year's Resolution was to get me in shape. That's pretty hardcore when you go about ruining someone else's day, making them run miles and miles. Turns out she wants to make a career out of it.

I finished my schedule. I'm taking four classes on MWF, and only one on TR. It starts late in the afternoon, so I can sleep like crazy on Monday and Wednesday nights. That's pretty cool, because I feel like sleeping alot lately.

I did something drastic for my New Year's resolution. I'm not going to share it here, so you'll have to ask me about it. It's really kind of funny, if you know me.

Ok, are you happy now? I posted.

Monday, January 10, 2005

The Last Blog

This is the first official Blog post for Spring 2005. I apologize that it's so late, but I suppose better late than never. I've had to update everything and shift from a single directory website to a more folder-organized website. I hope everyone notices a difference.

Alot of my webpages have been shut down though. The Friends Page and Galleries Page are down, but they should be up again within the month. I plan for my next update (which I don't even consider a tradition update) to be an ongoing one. My final version of my website which will remain forever in cyberspace will be a beautiful disertation of my four years at UGA. So stay tuned!

As for the new Blog, expect for at the very least, hopefully, weekly updates. I don't plan on writing nearly as often as I used to. I'll have the Fall 04 archive up very soon, so check there for the reason why. Otherwise, you know where to find me...if it's not already obvious, I'm always online!

Thanks for checking out the new Blog!
