Monday, June 13, 2005

Face Quake

I am concerned. I think, in light of running 5 days in a row last week, I've been undergoing some changes. Admittedly, this has me on edge. I've noticed that my face is changing shape. Of course, all semester it's been losing weight, but lately it's like my face looks like tectonic plates or something. What's up with that? I'm not Pangea!

Anyways, I'm just being goofy. Not about the running, just about the face stuff. My foot really hurts from all this running, though. That kind of sucks. I think it will go away, though. A few months ago, I had nasty pain in my knee that had me on edge. I eventually ran through it, and now it's fine. It was weird, too. It was the knee that never bothers me.

I changed the front page picture to reflect my shifting facial land mass phenomenon. What do you think?

Maybe I'm just a doof. Whatever.

Tonight is the Terror Revisited. Gen's spending another Monday night in Commerce so I have to puppy-sit so Kali doesn't eat more of Gen's carpet in her apartment. Stupid puppies.

Check out a picture of the Terror in my new summer photo album. Maybe I'll add more photos when I feel like it.

I think I'll take a nap.
